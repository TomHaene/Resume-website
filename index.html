BCD adder

Made using: The Altera FPGA, Quartus Prime Software and VHDL

Involved Creating a BCD adder using VHDL and a sevent-segment code converter, so that I could display the inputs and sum on the Altera board. The left two digits represent the operands, and the rightmost two digits represent the sum. Two digits were given to the sum due to overflow potential.
